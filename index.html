Atmosphere

Contains the air we breathe. Made of oxygen, nitrogen, and other gases. Creates weather and protects us from space.

Hydrosphere

All water on Earth - oceans, rivers, lakes, rain, and ice. Water moves through the water cycle.

Geosphere

The solid part of Earth including rocks, mountains, soil, and minerals. Always changing through erosion.

Biosphere

All living things - plants, animals, bacteria, and humans. Life exists in air, water, and on land.

How They Work Together

Water Cycle: Water evaporates from oceans, forms clouds, then falls as rain
Plant Growth: Plants need air, water, soil nutrients, and sunlight to grow
Weather: Air temperature and water vapor create different weather patterns
Erosion: Wind and water slowly wear down rocks and change the land
